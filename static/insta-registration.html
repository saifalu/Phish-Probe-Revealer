Empowering Minority Students for Success in GATE and ISRO Centralized Recruitment Board (ICRB) Examinations

Dear Aspiring Scholars,

We understand the tremendous dedication and elation that comes with achieving your academic goals. At Atiya Foundation, we are pleased to announce our new initiative aimed at supporting students pursuing their undergraduate and postgraduate studies while aspiring to excel in the GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) and ISRO Centralized Recruitment Board (ICRB) examinations, with the ultimate goal of securing admissions into prestigious institutions and organizations like IITs, ISRO, DRDO, and BARC.

Atiya Foundation has a longstanding history of providing unwavering assistance, including financial aid, educational resources, and mentorship, to Civil Services aspirants since 2010, leading to remarkable accomplishments. The resounding success of our Civil Services program has inspired us to expand our horizons and introduce a new program: "Kamiyab 2025."

In the Kamiyab 2025 program, we offer:

  • Free Mentorship: Guidance from accomplished candidates who have already successfully navigated these examinations.

  • Daily Lectures with Daily Practice Papers (DPPs): Engage in structured learning with daily lectures and practice papers to hone your skills and knowledge.

  • Practice Tests: Evaluate your progress and readiness through a series of practice tests designed to simulate the actual examination conditions.

  • Scholarships for Educational Materials: Financial assistance for the purchase of essential reference materials, specifically available to Economically Weaker Section (EWS) students.

This program is exclusively designed for students belonging to minority communities who are scheduled to complete their undergraduate or postgraduate programs in Science or Technology by 2025. Admission to the program operates on a first-come, first-serve basis.

To become a part of this transformative journey, we invite you to register for the program today. Rest assured, our team will reach out to you promptly after your registration to guide you further.

Atiya Foundation has earned a stellar reputation in the realm of civil service examinations, and our track record speaks volumes about our commitment to academic excellence.
